2ekmek1sut.com (Closed Down)
During the summer holiday of 2008, I decided to create a humor website and opened 2ekmek1sut.com. There were a lot of funny messages, drawings, videos, flash animations, games and a chat room, a music player, etc. I was also personally drawing cartoons and making flash animations too. It was a quite funny place for me.

I created a bot to greet new members. It was talking with the users in some cases. Additionally, a guard bot was always online for security, and a postman character was used for private messages. Members were throwing digital tomatoes at each other's messages if they don't like the messages or for just joking. There were also some special events for summer (throwing water balloons), April Fool's (having permission to ban other users for just a few minutes), etc. Members were also competing with each other in the gaming rooms.

At the start, there were around 100 loyal forum members. However, the rise of the social media sites like Facebook, Twitter, etc., affected all forum sites and the site turned into an almost empty place. In addition to this, the website was mostly using Adobe Flash for its interactive services. The web browsers started to block Adobe Flash player. After all of these, I shut down 2ekmek1sut.com in 2019 and transferred it in 2023.

Important: I transferred 2ekmek1sut.com in 2023. It no longer belongs to me.
